Our Story
Creating an iconic coffee blend

In 1978, as demand for their coffee grew they moved to their iconic roastery on Old Paradise Street. It was here the Costa brothers blind-tested 112 variations of coffee before they tasted one good enough to be our signature blend. Demand for Costa Coffee continued to grow as we sold our coffee beans to prestigious hotels in London, such as The Ritz, delivered in style by famous London black cabs.

The shop across the pond

In 1981, the brothers opened their first Good Coffee shop in Vauxhall Bridge Road and our reputation for artisan quality coffee increased dramatically. Shops opened across London, Europe and crossed continents into Asia and the Middle East. There are now over 4,000 Costa Coffee shops and 10,000 Smart Cafe machines worldwide. Costa is still consistently voted the UK's favorite coffee shop! And now you can enjoy the iconic, rich blends for the first time here in the U.S.A.
